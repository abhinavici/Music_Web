Help Section

Welcome to the help section of our music player application! Here you can find answers to frequently asked questions, troubleshooting tips, and contact information for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add music to my library?
    You can add music by clicking on the "Library" tab and then selecting "Add Music". You can upload files from your computer or import from supported streaming services.
  • How do I create a playlist?
    To create a playlist, go to the "Library" tab, click on "Create Playlist", and then add your desired songs to the playlist.
  • How do I adjust the settings?
    You can adjust settings by clicking on the "Settings" tab. Here you can customize your preferences such as theme, audio quality, and notifications.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • If you are experiencing issues with playback, try restarting the application or checking your internet connection.
  • If you cannot find a specific song in your library, ensure that it is properly added and that you have the correct permissions to access it.
  • If you encounter any bugs or glitches, please report them through the contact form below.
